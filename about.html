About Us

Founded in 2008, Color Universe Drive has become a leader in the car care chemicals sector thanks to its manufacturing technologies and innovative product range. Today, the company sells its patented products to many global companies in the Original Equipment Manufacturers market and maintains its growth rate through investments in sustainable production technologies, R & D and human resources.

Color Universe developed, patented and launched the world’s first ‘self-apply’ touch-up paint technology, which is almost the same quality as the original car paint.

Color Universe is managed by experts and technology experts around the world who are committed to car care chemicals and paints. We are proud to partner with all the global paint manufacturers in the automotive sector. Our success lies in our strong technology and customer focus. Working closely with paint manufacturers, we are constantly developing new touch-up paint systems with a focus on development.

We have a strong reputation for innovation, superior detailing technology, quality and service. Our team members are in the EU and the US for business development and sales support.

Our technology provides unique and environmentally friendly detailing solutions for perfect factory color matching and high quality. In order to ensure color harmony and original factory quality, we formulate our paints and chemicals with the most advanced mixing processes. We achieved the balance between low volatile organic compounds and high performance not only in our touch-up paints, but also in our additives.
